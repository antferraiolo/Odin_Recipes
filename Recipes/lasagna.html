Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna to share, brought to you by italians who care>/p>

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Tomato Sauce, lightly
  2. Boil Water
  3. Once water is boiling, add lasagna
  4. Once lasagna is soft, place in tray
  5. Layer tray with lasagna, soft, cheese, repeated
  6. Bake in oven
